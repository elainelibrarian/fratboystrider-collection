## About the Artist

Strider has a BFA in Comics & Narrative Practice from the Columbus College of Art and Design. He was born and raised in Borikén, Puerto Rico, where he fell in love with the horror genre and vampires. His work reflects his multicultural Caribbean upbringing, love for the gothic and macabre, and involvement in alternative and queer subcultures. The pilot of his original comic *Heartstrings* is available to read for free on [Webtoon](https://www.webtoons.com/en/canvas/heartstrings-pilot/list?title_no=994134). Visit [his website](https://fratboystrider.carrd.co/) for links to his active social media accounts, curated digital portfolios, and more.

## About the Collection

The collection provides a small, recent sample of Strider's physical art. Objects are sourced from Strider's sketchbooks and other loose materials, showcasing a range of artistic techniques, media, themes, and genres. All scans made accessible in this repository are included with the explicit permission and oversight of the creator.
